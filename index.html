Welcome!

Welcome to the website for our Aerospace Group Design Project, a unit part of our degree in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Manchester. On this website, you can learn more about our team and our project.

Our Project

Our project is focused on designing and building an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) for remote sensing applications. We are working together to develop the airframe, control system, and payload for the UAV.
